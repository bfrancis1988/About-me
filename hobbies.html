Frisbee Golf

One of my favorite things to do when it finally warms up and the sun comes out is to play frisbee golf.
Frisbee golf rules are pretty much what you would think. You use a fisbee and make it into the goal.
The goal is a pole with chains haning down and a basket under the chains. You try to make it in as little attempts as possible.
I like to be outside in the sun. Pretty much as long as it is outside I like it.
